## 2026-04-09 Research Report

### New Topic Recommendations

1. **程式設計師笑話 / Programmer Jokes** (slug: `programmer-jokes`, category: Humor)
   - Very strong niche community in Taiwan; evergreen content on PTT joke board, Dcard funny board, TechOrange, INSIDE
   - Classic format: logic/literal thinking jokes (the "5 apples" joke is iconic and universally recognized among tech workers)
   - High shareability in LINE group chats among office workers and students
   - SEO targets: 工程師笑話, 程式設計師笑話, IT笑話, 工程師語錄
   - Content pool: large — logic jokes, bug/debugging jokes, programmer stereotypes, tech puns

2. **星座語錄 / Zodiac Quotes** (slug: `zodiac-quotes`, category: Social)
   - Consistently high search volume year-round, peaks around new year and Valentine's
   - 2026 content hook: annual horoscope predictions driving fresh searches ("2026星座運勢")
   - Especially popular among female users 18–35; strong on LINE Today, Marie Claire TW
   - Sub-formats: zodiac personality traits, dating compatibility lines, zodiac "幹話"
   - SEO targets: 星座語錄, 12星座, 星座特質, 星座情話

3. **幹話語錄 / Sarcastic/Absurd Quotes** (slug: `witty-comebacks`, category: Humor)
   - Z-generation internet culture trend: "活人感", "低山臭水遇知音", "情緒價值" all trending in 2025–2026
   - High engagement on Dcard and PTT among 18–25 demographic
   - Format: short, punchy, self-deprecating or absurdist one-liners
   - Differentiated from cold-jokes by tone (sardonic vs. punny)
   - SEO targets: 幹話語錄, 搞笑語錄, 金句, 廢話文學

4. **勵志語錄 / Motivational Quotes** (slug: `motivational-quotes`, category: Motivation)
   - Evergreen, broad appeal across all demographics
   - High search volume ("語錄 2026", "激勵語錄", "正能量語錄")
   - Works well for back-to-school season (Sept), year-end (Dec), New Year (Jan)
   - Differentiator: FunTextHub should focus on "輕勵志" (light motivation) — witty and relatable, not preachy
   - SEO targets: 勵志語錄, 正能量語錄, 人生金句, 激勵短句
